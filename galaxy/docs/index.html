Easy, modern, and straightforward layouts

Galaxy packages Flexbox, custom attributes, and out-of-the-box performance optimization so you can focus fully on design rather than the complexities of CSS.

Lightning fast

Most of Galaxy is handled in CSS, making motion ui feeling incredibly smooth and native. In fact, Galaxy.js is only 3kb minified!

Built on design specs

Galaxy follows a variation of the Google's Design Specs, making the framework consistent, beautiful, and legibly crafted.

Semantic markup

By using the g attribute, there are literally no naming constraints. This means no prefixing, overriding styles, or messy markup.
